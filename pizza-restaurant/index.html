“Magic of Pizza” is
the origin of our restaurant.

Back in 2005, the founder of Pizza, Masuko, built a wood-fired pizza oven with his friends in the backyard of his house. He invited his friends and family and hosted pizza parties.

In 2011, the original members who once made the pizza oven gathered again and started the history of Pizza. We named our restaurant Pizza, based on our vision, Make the World Smile "For Peace."
